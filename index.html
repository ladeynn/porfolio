Cursed by Magic -
Secret Library

A barroque enviroment created for the videogame Cursed by Magic: Warlock Arcanum.

The setting to be designed is a clandestine room inside the Magic Academy. In the past, it was used as a meeting place for a secret society that tried to decipher the origin of the magic that reigned in the world, but it seems that no one has been there for a long time.

Lighting played a crucial role in this scene. TThe subtle disarray of the room suggests that the last occupants had to leave in a hurry without looking back.

Catpuccino -
Coffee Shop

Environment created for the game "Catpuccino". Modeled and textured in collaboration with Ángela Sánchez.

I developed the concept for the scene based on the ideas Ángela and I had, always aiming to convey a cozy and endearing atmosphere, typical of cozy games.

The color palette is dominated by pastel colours, such as pinks, browns, creams and soft yellows, which channel the calm and sweetness that the game emits.

Witch's Office

A personal project focused on storytelling through props.

The setting is the witch's private study. The study has two floors: the main floor and a lookout on the ground floor. It is divided into different areas for various fields of study (potions, art, etc.), with plenty of bookshelves for learning, and a relaxation area. The walls are made of gray stone with wooden accents at the corners.

The scene is set in one of the rooms of a castle in a kingdom, in a high fantasy world where magic exists. The final render will be taken in the morning.

Argider
Antagonist

Argider is a 17-year-old elf who attends the Academy of Magic. He comes from a prestigious family in this magic world and grew up under strict discipline. The pressure from his mother, a professor at the Academy, led him to be a perfectionist, proud, and obsessed with excellence.

Tall and slender, with impeccably styled ash-blond hair and blue-gray eyes, he maintains an elegant posture and impeccable attire. Strategic and competitive, he plans every step with precision and rarely acts impulsively. Although reserved and distant, he has a strong sense of duty and protects those he cares about, even if he never expresses it openly.

Dorcas -
The Witch

Dorcas McKinnon is a 18 years old witch. She is a Black woman of average height with black hair and eyes. Her Afro hair is styled in locs and buns made with elastic bands. She wears a button-down vest and a shirt over it, and pants with boots.

Dorcas is a calm but strong-willed person who loves to learn. She is also a worldly woman, having traveled to different kingdoms to learn about diverse cultures. When she's not traveling, she can be found in her study, provided by the queen of the castle. The queen has entrusted her with the task of finding a cure to save her daughter. Dorcas also loves to read and paint, which explains the large collection of books and an easel in her study. Those who know her describe her as a very beautiful person, both inside and out.

Teenager's -
Messy Room

A contemporary environment focusing on environmental storytelling through clutter.

This room belongs to a chaotic teenager who loves blue. The challenge was to make the mess feel natural and lived-in, scattering personal objects, clothes, and posters to define the character's personality without showing them.

The lighting is soft daylight, emphasizing the cool blue tones.

Baroque
Elf Dormitory

A fantasy environment blending baroque architecture with magical elements for an elven academy.

The color palette is rich in deep greens, purples, and gold accents, conveying luxury and ancient magic. Ornate furniture, magical plants, and glowing runes fill the space.

I focused on detailed texturing for the wood carvings and gold trims to achieve an opulent baroque feel.

Cemetery at Dawn
at Dawn

An atmospheric exterior scene capturing the specific lighting of sunrise.

The composition features an old church and a prominent weathered statue. The goal was to capture the quiet, melancholic beauty of the church, caught in the early sun rays.

Heavy use of organic modeling for foliage and weathered stone texturing.

Aegis -
Main character

Character design for a fantasy RPG project.

Aegis is a master's student at Blackmoor University, a prestigious institution in a world where magic and technology coexist. From a young age, he displayed an innate talent for magic, which led to his admission to the Applied Arcane Arts program.

However, after an accident during a forbidden experiment, Aegis was marked with a curse that consumes his body every time he uses magic. In his quest for a cure, he delves into the city's occult underworld, confronting cults, creatures from other dimensions, and his own decay.

James -
Catpuccino Customer

NPC designed for the "Catpuccino" coffee shop.

The outfit features soft fabrics and oversized accessories to emphasize the cute and relaxing vibe of the game.

James is a customer with wavy black hair. He wears a loose-fitting pastel green sweater that reveals a shirt collar underneath. His trousers are brown. He is an extroverted and expressive character; his emotions are clearly reflected on his face.

I used a slightly more muted color palette (creams and browns) to connect them visually with the coffee elements of the environment.

N.I.C.A.N.
Squad

A character lineup designed for a 4v4 "Capture the Flag" multiplayer game set in space.

The goal was to create 4 distinct classes (Tank, Support, DPS, Mechanic) the main goal was to create distinct heroes for a First-Person perspective. Silhouette readability was priority #1: players need to identify the enemy class instantly during fast-paced combat.

While they share a cohesive sci-fi and steampunk technology, each character has unique shape language and color accents to visually communicate their abilities and role within the team.

Cursed by Magic
UI / UX

User Interface designed for the game "Cursed by Magic".

The goal was to translate the game's baroque aesthetic into the interface. I used ornate gold frames, dark parchment textures, and magical glowing effects to make the UI feel diegetic—part of the wizard's world.

Key Elements: Spell selection wheel, inventory system, and dynamic health/mana bars inspired by liquid potions.

Nican
Tactical UI

User Interface for "Nican", the 4v4 tactical shooter project.

Following the gameplay needs of a competitive shooter (inspired by Valorant), the UI prioritizes clarity and instant readability. The aesthetic is clean, sharp, and futuristic.

Design Focus: Minimizing visual noise in the HUD so players can focus on aiming, while using distinct color coding (Teal vs Red) for Ally/Enemy recognition.
